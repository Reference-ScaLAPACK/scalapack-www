3. ScaLAPACK 2.1: What's new
------------------------------

ScaLAPACK 2.1 contains:
 
- *New robust ScaLAPACK routine for computing the QR factorization with column pivoting* by Zvonimir Bujanovic and Zlatko Drmac [link:http://www.netlib.org/lapack/lawnspdf/lawn296.pdf[Download Lawn 296]]

 - MPI-3+ updates required by Open MPI 4

 - Improve accuracy of Frobenius norm by making intermediate column sums
 
 - miscellaneous minor bug fixes

 - Improve cmake shared library support
